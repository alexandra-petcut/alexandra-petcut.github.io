About Me

I'm Alexandra, a third-year student at the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca. My interest in software engineering began with my first lines of code, an experience that sparked my fascination with the field's versatility.

I believe that with creativity, persistence and passion, every idea can be turned into a functional, real-world application. I enjoy analyzing complex problems and breaking them down to design efficient solutions. I find great satisfaction in building systems step by step and seeing steady progress is especially rewarding to me.

As a fast-learner and a detail-oriented individual, I am determined to grow and learn from experienced engineers.
